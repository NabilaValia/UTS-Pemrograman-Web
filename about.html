ABOUT ME

Creator | Content & Copywriting | Editing Video

Hi! My name is Nabila Valia, but you can just call me Valia. I’m a content creator who loves exploring and promoting hidden gems around Malang — from cozy cafés and local eateries to unique hangout spots.

Creating engaging and relatable content is my way of helping people discover new experiences and supporting local businesses. With a creative touch and a passion for storytelling, I turn every place I visit into an inspiring visual journey that connects with my audience.
